## Common

- You have a page /profile - the user's page_, /search - the graph and user search page_, /g.id - _the graph_ page
- Hover over the icon to see its brief description

## Pages

#### /profile
here you can change your username, avatars and "about yourself"
- Click on `save profile` to update the information for everyone
- Click on `log out` to log out

#### /search
Here you can find the course you want to study
- Click on `course briefcard` to access the course page
- Enter your query to find the course
- Switch between the icons to the left of the query input to choose which to search for: users or courses
- Press `+g` to create your own course
